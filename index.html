About Our School

Welcome to Mirembe Nursery and Primary School

Established in 2005, Mirembe Nursery and Primary School has been dedicated to providing exceptional education to children in Kirinya Bweyogerere and surrounding communities. Our name "Mirembe" means peace, reflecting our commitment to creating a harmonious learning environment.

Qualified Teachers

Our educators are highly trained and passionate about child development.

Nurturing Environment

We provide a safe, inclusive space where every child can thrive.

Holistic Curriculum

Balancing academics with creative and physical development.

Community Focus

Strong partnerships with parents and the local community.

Admissions

Join Our School Community

We welcome applications for all classes throughout the year, subject to availability. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

To apply, please complete the inquiry form or visit our school during working hours. Our administration team will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

Requirements for Admission:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school reports (for transfers)
  • Medical form and immunization records
  • Passport-sized photographs

We offer affordable tuition fees with flexible payment options and scholarships for exceptional students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
