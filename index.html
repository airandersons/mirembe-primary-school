About Our School

Welcome to Mirembe Nursery and Primary School

Established in 2005, Mirembe Nursery and Primary School has been providing quality education to children in Kirinya Bweyogerere and surrounding communities. Our name "Mirembe" means peace, which reflects our commitment to creating a peaceful and conducive learning environment.

We offer both nursery and primary education with a curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development. Our dedicated teachers are committed to nurturing each child's unique talents and abilities.

At Mirembe, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we work closely with parents to ensure holistic development for our students.

Admissions

Join Our School Community

We welcome applications for all classes throughout the year, subject to availability. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

To apply, please complete the inquiry form or visit our school during working hours. Our administration team will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.

We offer affordable tuition fees with flexible payment options and scholarships for exceptional students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Requirements for Admission:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school reports (for transfers)
  • Medical form and immunization records
  • Passport-sized photographs
